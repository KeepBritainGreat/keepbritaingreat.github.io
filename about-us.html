About Us


We are a few ordinary Brits who love our country, and who want to help convince our fellow citizens that we should remain members of the European Union.

  • We believe that other campaigns still haven't learnt the lessons from the 2016 referendum.
  • We believe that keeping our message positive is the best way to win people over.
  • We believe that campaigning to change people's minds should happen before campaigning for a second referendum.
  • We believe there is still time.
  • We believe that the left and moderates should reclaim patriotism from the far-right.
  • We believe that campaigning for a course of action that won't see our country badly weakened is the most patriotic thing we can do.
